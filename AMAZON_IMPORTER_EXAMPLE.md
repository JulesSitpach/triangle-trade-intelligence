### Step 2: Shipment from Mexico to US

### Step 3: Amazon Customs Clearance
- Amazon's customs broker receives USMCA certificate
- Files CBP Form 7501 (Entry Summary)
- Claims USMCA preference (Special Program Indicator = "MX")
- Duty Rate: **0%** (instead of standard 2.6% MFN rate)

### Step 4: Duty Savings
- Standard MFN duty: 2.6% Ã— $8,500,000 = **$221,000**
